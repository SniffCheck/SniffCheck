SniffCheck Web Flasher

RF environment audit tool, plus the Dog Park firmware family.

Flash a SniffCheck board straight from your browser. No ESP-IDF, no Python, no drivers. Pick a firmware, plug the matching board into a USB port, click Install, choose the serial port, and wait.

This browser can't flash over USB. Use Chrome or Edge on a desktop (Windows, macOS, or Linux). Firefox, Safari, and all phones/tablets lack the Web Serial API. 



  

    If the dongle isn't detected or flashing fails: unplug it,
    hold the BOOT button, plug it back in while still holding,
    then release and click Install again. This forces download mode.
  


  
What it does

  

    SniffCheck passively audits the Wi-Fi (both bands) and BLE around you and shows a
    safety verdict on its little screen. It only listens — it never connects to
    or transmits to other devices, and nothing leaves the dongle. The flash
    includes the firmware and the vendor identification database, so it scans
    correctly on first boot.
